My name is Leopolina and i am 29 years old. I was born in Split,Croatia where i spent the first ten years of my life. Then i moved to San Jose , California with my mom when i was 11. I knew no Engish and this great big new world was intiminating, but pretty soon i got used to it and grew to love it. I went to middle school, high school and then it came time for college. I always knew i wanted to do something oftware related so i gave Software Engineering a shot but all the math, science, ect classes that are required stoped me from pursuing it further. I stopped attending community college and at about 24 years old decided to go back to Europe. This time to Podgorica,Montenegro. I have family here so it was a no brainer. I landed in Frankfurt, Germany and drove with my dad all the way to Montenegro.In October it will be 8 years that i have moved here. At first i was lost and didnt know what i wanted to do. Eventually i found my way and started teaching English as a second language online. I have been doing that for 5 years now. While i love helping others and teaching i found myself underpaid and burnt out and in need of a change. I started working on music production and my music which is also a huge part od my life but i always wondered how i could tie that in with my love for coding. I started exploring my options to get back into coding and by chance one of my good friends sent me a link to Udacity and their scholorship program. I decided to apply, after all i had nothing to loose but a lot to gain. Now here i am coding my first website and it is scary and exciting at the same time. Looking at that white page and blank code , there is nothing worse, but once you get started you just cant seem to stop, and there is no better feeling than seeing that page work like it is supposed to for the first time. So who am i? Well hopefull on my way to becoming an amazing Front End Developer. Heres to the future.
